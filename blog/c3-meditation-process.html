Cultural Blog Part 2

  • What did you think about meditation before reading chapter 2?

    • I always thought it was a lifestyle choice which I could never really get my head around but I think that is due to a lack of knowledge.

  • What new things have you learnt about meditation?

    • That you have to start to become aware of your own distraction and learn to bring yourself back to the state of focus. Repeat and teach your body/mind to learn.

  • Did any of the suggested meditation techniques stand out to you?

    • That you can really meditate in any posture you want and you can find your own way that works for you.

  • Any other musings?

    • I've always thought that playing piano was my own way of 'meditating'. I have to play now and again just to kind of keep me feeling good overall. It used to be gaming or guitar but it has evolved into piano.
