Reflection Blog

  • What's your take on the DBC/EDA experience

    • I haven't done a course like this before. I have zero experience in this area so learning in a way where you have to seek solutions by many different means is both challenging and rewarding.

  • What are your impressions?

    • Exciting, different, progressive.

  • How do you see yourself engaging with this type of culture?

    • I'm very open minded and willing to learn so I think it will be beneficial to all parties hopefully.

  • Have your expectations of EDA changed? If so, how?

    • I guess it's still early days so I'm not really sure. Ask me again in a few weeks !

  • Are you excited to participate in this kind of learning environment? Does it make you nervous?

    • I always get nervous but I like to think that makes me thrive. I am excited but just hope I can keep up !
